Six Frameworks

RENKAI Career Science
in action.

RENKAI Career Science is our proprietary approach to career discovery and life design. We combine reverse & forward engineering principles with opportunity mapping and excellence frameworks to help students move from uncertainty to clarity.

Reverse Engineering Excellence

We start with the desired future and work backwards — mapping every decision, milestone, and skill required to reach it with precision and intent.

Forward Engineering Pathways

We help students project forward from who they are today — identifying emerging opportunities that align with their strengths and deepest passions.

Career Pattern Recognition

Using years of student journey data, we identify what actually works — not what's assumed — in building meaningful, sustainable careers.

Opportunity Mapping

We map a student's passion against global problem landscapes — connecting them to the geography, institution, and community best suited for their mission.

Excellence Frameworks

Drawn from Svadhyaya & Svadharma — our published frameworks build self-awareness as the living foundation of every career decision we guide.

Life Design Architecture

Beyond career planning — values, worldview, purpose, and identity form the bedrock of every recommendation we make. We help students design a life.
