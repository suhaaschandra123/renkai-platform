The Partnership

career intelligence team.

RENKAI partners with schools to build structured, end-to-end career guidance systems for students from Grades 8 through 12. We are present on campus, available online, and invested in every student's future as deeply as you are.

Two on-campus visits per year. Year-round online counseling. Individualised career reports. University fairs. Parent sessions. Everything a student needs — from self-discovery to final university application.
